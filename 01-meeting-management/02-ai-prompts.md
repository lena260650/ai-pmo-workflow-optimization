# AI Prompt Examples — Meeting Summary Generation
## Prompt Example 1 — Meeting Summary Generator

### Input Prompt (paste into ChatGPT / Claude)

You are an executive assistant AI.

Convert the following meeting notes into a structured summary:

- Key decisions
- Action items
- Risks / blockers
- Next steps

Rules:
- Do not invent information
- If something is unclear, mark as TBD
- Keep output concise and structured

Meeting Notes:
Here is a polished and more professional version of your meeting notes:

---

## MEETING AGENDA

1. Review of the results of the **“Reengineering of Project A Resource Management Process”** project, including:

   1.1. Centralization of Project A contract administration functions
   1.2. Process standardization and transition to a unified Project A contract template
   1.3. Automation of the target process in the context of IT system import substitution
   1.4. SLA performance trends and measures to improve process quality
   1.5. Implementation of budget and headcount planning tools
   1.6. Process monetization and development of an external staffing product

2. Discussion of proposed solutions for key project areas and review of the initiative to develop an external product.

---

## DECISIONS MADE

### 1. Centralization of Contract Administration Functions

**1.1.** Approve the rollout plan for implementing the target process across the remaining business areas in accordance with the established schedule. Ensure ongoing monitoring and execution.

**Responsible:** Thomas, Jessica
**Deadline:** October 15, 2026

**1.2.** Develop a business process framework that incorporates all required controls and validation checks. Upon completion, conduct pilot testing. The topic of full contract centralization will be revisited only after successful pilot completion.

**Responsible:** Thomas, Jessica
**Deadline:** October 15, 2026

---

### 2. Automation of the Target Process in the Context of IT System Import Substitution

**2.1.** Evaluate options for seamless migration to a unified electronic document management system in order to minimize risks related to process quality deterioration and resource constraints.

---

### 3. Process Monetization and External Product Development

**3.1.** Conduct a Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) assessment to support decision-making regarding scenarios for the development and scaling of the external product.

**Responsible:** Thomas, Jessica
**Deadline:** October 15, 2026

---

### 4. Implementation of Budget and Headcount Planning Tools

**4.1.** By October 1, 2026, transition to full-scale contract governance covering budget, headcount, and functional oversight.
By July 15, 2026, update the driver models for B2B Contract Types 1 and 2, and align B2B Contract Type 3 with target benchmarks (including contract termination or renewal in accordance with the updated reference guide and budget approved by the Management Committee in October 2025).

**Responsible:** Samantha
**Deadline:** August 15, 2026

**4.2.** In cooperation with the Finance and Economics Department and the B2B business segment, develop a governance framework defining the balance of authority for budget and external workforce management.

**Responsible:** Samantha
**Deadline:** September 1, 2026

---

### 5. Next Meeting

The next meeting will be held in October 2026.

**Meeting Summary**
- Discussed Q2 delivery timeline and resource constraints
- Agreed to prioritize recruitment automation module

**Key Decisions**
